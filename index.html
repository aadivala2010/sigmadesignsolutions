About Us

Welcome to Sigma Design Solutions.

At Sigma Design Solutions Consulting, we empower small and medium-sized businesses with strategic, results-driven engineering and business consulting services. With a team of seasoned engineers and industry experts, we bring deep experience, insight, and innovation to every engagement, helping clients identify opportunities, streamline operations, and achieve sustainable growth.

We specialize in CAD design engineering, simulation, prototype manufacturing, quality systems, operations management, and strategic consultations, delivering end-to-end solutions that transform ideas into tangible results.

What sets us apart is our commitment to customized solutions tailored to each client's unique challenges and goals. We build long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and measurable success, with solutions focused on cost-efficiency and high-impact results.

With a growing global presence and offices in both the United States and India, we support clients across industries and continents while staying true to our mission: to deliver smart, scalable, and sustainable solutions that drive meaningful growth.

What we do

Engineering clarity from concept through production.

Our mechanical design and consulting services support companies that need practical engineering judgment, clear documentation, and dependable execution. We create and refine mechanical systems, machinery, fixtures, product components, and operational workflows using modern design tools.

With experience across CAD design engineering, simulation, prototype manufacturing, quality systems, operations management, and strategic consultation, Sigma Design Solutions brings structure to the work so your team can move with confidence.

  • Mechanical design solutions for the creation and optimization of mechanical systems and components.
  • Engineering principles applied to design, analyze, and refine structures, machinery, and products.

  • Support for new products, existing machinery improvements, prototype work, testing, and manufacturing needs.
